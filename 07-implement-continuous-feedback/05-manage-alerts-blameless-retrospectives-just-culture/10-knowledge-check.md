# Module Assessment

## Knowledge Check

This is an interactive assessment to test your understanding of:
- Azure Application Insights smart detection
- Performance degradation notifications
- Alert management best practices
- Blameless retrospectives
- Just culture principles

## Key Topics to Review

### Smart Detection
- How it identifies performance anomalies
- Three detection categories (response time, dependency, patterns)
- 8-day baseline establishment period
- Notification components (triage, scope, diagnostic info)

### Alert Management
- Principles for actionable alerts (urgent, important, actionable, real)
- Alert on symptoms, not causes
- Problem classification (availability, latency, correctness, feature-specific)
- Managing alert fatigue
- Separating urgency levels (page-worthy, next-day, informational)

### Blameless Retrospectives
- Why punishment prevents learning
- The cycle of blame and its consequences
- Local rationality (actions made sense at the time)
- What blameless doesn't mean
- Building psychological safety

### Just Culture
- Engineers as experts in their errors
- Seven principles for enabling Just Culture
- Blunt end vs. sharp end
- Learning vs. blame approaches
- Implementation practices

## Assessment Format
- Multiple-choice questions
- Tests comprehension of module content
- Instant feedback on answers
